EasyLife: Java Shopping Application

Behind the Development: EasyLife

EasyLife is a Java-based online shopping app designed to connect customers with their desired products globally. Developed during my final project at SUPMTI, it offers seamless browsing and purchasing experiences.

The app features two core modules: Navigation, for a smooth user interface, and Shopping, for browsing and purchasing products from various brands.

Built using Scrum methodology, the project focused on delivering a user-friendly, scalable solution, ensuring a seamless shopping experience through clear software architecture.

EasyLife combines my software development expertise with business insights to offer an accessible, real-time online shopping platform.
